Achievements

Youngest South-North Round Trip Motorcyclist

I set the record for being the youngest person to complete a round trip motorcycle expedition from South to North India. My journey began at Vidhaan Soudha, Bengaluru on April 30, 2023. I reached Lal Chowk, Srinagar on May 5, continued to Kanyakumari on May 12, and returned to Bengaluru on May 14, completing the expedition in just 15 days.

I covered a distance of 7638 km at the age of 18 years, 8 months, and 26 days. This achievement was officially recognized by the India Book of Records on May 23, 2023.
